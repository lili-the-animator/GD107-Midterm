Play Hades the Game - Gameplay

Gameplay

Hack-and-Slash

Hades features fast-paced, dynamic combat against hordes of enemies, with an emphasis on action and gear progression. There are six unique melee weapons and much more to unlock.

Earn Powerful Boons

Call upon the Olympian Gods and earn their favor. There are nine Gods to seek out and each bestows different kinds of abilities that may help you in your attempt to escape.

Repeat

Not even death can stop you as you fight your way out. If you should meet your end while attempting escape, return home through the River Styx and try again. No run is exactly the same, see how far your next escape attempt will take you.
